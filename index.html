Gallery

I have always been attracted to drawing since my childhood. My parents decided that the best option for me was to go to Art School. I was drawing at Art school for 11 years.

It was like a normal school with lessons in math, french, english, literature and others. However every week we had to draw for 18 hours at school and several hours at home. It was not only drawing lessons - we painted, did sculptures, ceramic objects. The homework we were assigned was not only for home, we drew at the zoo, at museums and in nature.

We were painting everywhere. It was very funny. I decided that drawing was a more of a hobby for me.

I was looking for something more challenging.

I went to University to learn math, engineering and economic sciences.

About me

My name is Diana. I am 29 years old. I graduated from the Institute of Steel and Alloys in Moscow with honors.

My first job was at an Audit company, I worked as an assistant to the auditor.

Then I worked as a leading analyst at the Government in the Office of the Mayor and at the Government of Moscow. I helped save up 1.2 million rubles for the staffs' training for 2 months. The Department bought software for analysis and forecast ("Prognoz platform"), but it did not include the cost of training. I learned and tested this software, taught my colleagues which helped save them money for training.

After this succesfull experience I decided work in IT. I worked as a QA engineer in Aplana LLC. I was the outsource staff in Sbertech, it is a Sberbank company for bank software development.

I also worked with Tech and Software: SQL, XML, IBM Jazz, Confluence.

"Sberbank of Russia" was our customer. It was a high loaded integration billing system (ESB IBM, Java, Oracle). Quality Assurance in a distributed agile team, acceptance testing and a demo for customers. I was doing functional testing of banks' systems such as 'Billing System' and 'Corporate System'. Clients did not find incidents after my work.

One year I worked as a product and project manager in small company named 'LAVINA PULSE'.

I worked with Tech and Software: Git.

Our team released the software for cinema's network "Cinema park". It analyzes competitors, this increases 5% of the cinema's revenue. We also developed a dashboard on the analysis of their activities, showing errors in the policy of film screenings.

I was learning for 8 months on MASA program about startups. I generated a lot of ideas, checked them and researched. I found a team, and we worked together.

I taught myself HTML, CSS, SQL and Database. I taught myself the basics of Python.
