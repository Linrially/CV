I have drawn since my childhood. My parents decided that I had to go to Art School. I was drawing at Art school for 11 years.

It was like a normal school with maths, french, english, literature and others lessons. But every week we had to draw for 18 hours at school and several hours at home to do a homework. It was not only draw lessons - we painted, drawed, did sculptures, ceramic objects. Our homework we had to do was not only at home: we were go to Zoo, to Museums, to a nature.

We were painting everywhere. It was very funny. I decided to left it like a hobby.

I wanted decide difficult tasks.

I went to University to learn maths, engineering and economic siences.

About me

My name is Diana. I am 29 years old. I graduated Institut of Steel and Alloys in Moscow with honors.

My first work was in Audit company, I worked an assistant to auditor.

Then I worked a leading analyst in the Government in the Office of the Mayor and the Government of Moscow. I saved up to 1.2 million rubles for staffs' training for 2 months. The Department bought software for analysis and forecast ("Prognoz platform"), but it did not buy training. I learnt and tested this software, taught of colleagues, that is I saved money for staffs' training.

After this succesfull experince I decided work in IT. I worked a QA engineer in Aplana LLC. I was autsource staff in Sbertech, it is Sberbank company for bank software development.

I worked with Tech and Software: SQL, XML, IBM Jazz, Confluence.

"Sberbank of Russia" was our customer. It was highLoaded integration billing system (ESB IBM, Java, Oracle). Quality Assurance in distributed agile team, acceptance testing, demo for customers. I was doing functional testing of banks' systems such as Billing system and Corporate system. Clients did not find incidents after my work.

One year I worked a product and project manager in small company LAVINA PULSE.

I worked with Tech and Software: Git.

Our team release the software for cinema's network "Cinema park". It analyzes competitors, this increases for 5% cinema's revenue. We also developed a dashboard on the analysis of their activities, showing errors in the policy of film screenings.

I was learning for 8 months on MASA program about startup. I generated a lot of ideas, cheked them and researched. I found a team, and we work together.

I learnt myself Python, HTML, CSS. And now I am studing SQL and Database.
